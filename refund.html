Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with Grammar Spy™. If it's not the right fit, we'll make it right.

Free Content

Mission 01 (Retell What Happened) is always free. No payment required, no refund needed. Try it before you buy.

Paid Subscriptions

If you purchased a subscription and are not satisfied:

  • Within 14 days: Full refund, no questions asked. Email us and we'll process it immediately.
  • After 14 days: We'll review your request on a case-by-case basis. We're teachers too — we understand.

School Licenses

School and district licenses can be refunded within 30 days of purchase if the product hasn't been deployed to students. Contact us to discuss.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@grammarspy.net with:

  • Your name and email used for the account
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund (optional — but helps us improve)

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days back to your original payment method.

Teacher Access Codes

Access provided through teacher classroom codes (like KANPER4) is free for students and does not involve a purchase. No refund applies.

Questions?

Contact us at support@grammarspy.net. We respond within 1–2 business days.
