What teachers learn in this video

  1. Pick Teacher mode on the homepage and continue to the dashboard.
  2. Open Missions, choose a pack, then select difficulty.
  3. Run one game round in Teacher Mode and review immediate feedback.
  4. Open reports/insights and decide the next mission step.
  5. Return to dashboard to continue, switch packs, or assign independent practice.
