Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

Grammar Spy™ collects minimal data to provide the service:

  • Account information: Name and email address (if you create an account)
  • Usage data: Game scores, accuracy, and progress — stored locally in your browser (localStorage)
  • Analytics: Anonymous page views via Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how the site is used

How Data Is Stored

All personal data (name, email, game progress) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Grammar Spy™ does not operate a backend server or database. Your data stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Collects anonymous usage statistics. No personally identifiable information is shared. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: If you make a payment, Stripe processes it securely. Grammar Spy™ never sees or stores your payment card details. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Hosts the website. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Grammar Spy™ is designed for classroom use with students aged 10+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental or school consent. Teacher accounts manage student access. If you believe a child's data was collected improperly, please contact us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Anonymous analytics data may be used to improve the product.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for grammarspy.net. Since data is stored locally, deleting it removes it permanently.

Cookies

Grammar Spy™ uses localStorage (not cookies) for account and progress data. Google Analytics may set cookies for anonymous tracking.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us at support@grammarspy.net.
