Frequently asked questions

What grammar activities work best for whole-class instruction?

Short mission rounds with one clear objective tend to work best. Teachers can model a round, run student turns, then debrief mistakes quickly.

Are online grammar games effective for middle and high school students?

They are most effective when games are paired with explicit goals and review. Grammar Spy is designed for this structure, not random play.

Can I use these grammar games for bell ringers or intervention?

Yes. Many teachers use missions as grammar bell ringers, warmups, and targeted intervention activities.

Which grammar skills can students practice online?

Students can practice tense control, agreement, sentence structure, parts of speech, and error correction.
